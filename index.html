Mines Backing LitiCoin

Our mine sites feature rich deposits of valuable mineral resources which includes Lithium, Tantalite, Gold, Tourmaline, Tin, Lead, Mica and Gem Stones, allowing us to meet the growing demands of various industries.

BAUCHI, TORO

BAUCHI, TORO Mine is rich in COPPER, LEAD, ZINC and LIHTIUM. Our license for exploration and development is covered for 119.4 Km2 Area. This site is located in Bauchi State, Nigeria.

DONGA, USSA

The mineral reserve in DONGA and USSA (BERYL, CALCITE, COLUMBITE, FLOURSPAR/FLOURITE, LITHIUM) is in mass value and covering an area of 47km2. We have the license and grant to harness the rich minerals, thereby assisting in the development of the community.

YAGBA WEST

The mass-valued mineral reserve in YAGBA WEST spans 22.8 km2. We are allowed to utilise our mining permit and grant to unearth the valuable minerals and contribute to the community's development.

BARUTEN, SAKI WEST

A 47 km2 mass-valued mineral field located in BARUTEN and SAKI WEST. The minerals located in this sites include LITHIUM, GOLD, CASSITERITE, TOURMALINE and TANTALUM.

JEMA'A

We have the required license harness the minerals in the JEMA'A georaphy. Our exploration grant covers 59.4Km2 of JEMA'A area.

OGBADIBO, ISI UZO, NSUKKA, UDENU

These areas has mass deposit of CLAY, COAL & KOALINE. The area covered is 139Km2.

Frequently Asked Questions

The Liticoin FAQ offers concise answers to common inquiries about the project, covering topics such as its purpose, technology, tokenomics, and roadmap.

1. What is LITI Token?

LITI Token is a blockchain-based asset backed by the real-world mining operations of Lithium King in Nigeria. It represents ownership in tangible assets like lithium and other strategic metals.

2. Who is Lithium King?

Lithium King is a well-established mining company operating in Nigeria. Led by CEO Mr. David Cohen with over 30 years experience, the company has a successful track record in both liquid and solid mineral mining across Africa.

3. Can American Citizens take part in the Crowd Sale?

No, American citizens are only allowed to purchase LITI in private sale and covered by US security Regulation S.

4. Does the Crowd Sale comply with legal regulations?

Yes, Lithium King is registered entity in all the location it operates and adhering to the requlatory requirements.

5. Can i trade LITI at an exchange?

After the crowdsale, LITI will be launched on three major decentralized exchanges.
